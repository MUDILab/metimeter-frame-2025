The metimeter (from metis, skill, and meter) is an online calculator aimed at helping serious practitioners in evaluating AI-based decision support systems by examining key areas like data reliability, model robustness, calibration, utility, and AI’s effect on human decisions.

Project management 2025: Nicolò Paparella and Luca Polonia, under the scientific supervision of Prof. Federico Cabitza (federico.cabitza [ at ] unimib.it) and the technical supervision of Ing. Angelo Gordini (angelo.gordini [ at ] gmail.com).
